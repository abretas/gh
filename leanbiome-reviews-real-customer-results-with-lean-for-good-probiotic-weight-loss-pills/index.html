The presence of these bacteria helps maintain a healthy balance of microflora in the gut, which can help reduce inflammation throughout the body.

Lactobacillus rhamnosus is a lactic acid-producing bacterium that is commonly found in fermented foods. It belongs to the genus Lactobacillus which includes over 100 different species of bacteria.
