Micro-Encapsulated Calcium

Micro-Encapsulated Calcium is a form of calcium created through nanotechnology. Tiny particles are coated with an ultra-thin protective layer in order to contain them within a solution and released gradually into your mouth when triggered by gustation or physical contact with your teeth and gums.
What makes MEC special is that the particles remain intact until they reach the target area, allowing them adequate time to interact with the environment before being washed away.

Xylitol is a naturally-occurring sugar alcohol found in numerous fruits and vegetables that looks and tastes like regular sugar but offers fewer calories and less of an impact on blood glucose levels.
