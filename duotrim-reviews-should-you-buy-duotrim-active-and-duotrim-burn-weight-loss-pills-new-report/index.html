How Does Science Back DuoTrim? – An Overview Of Studies Backing Probiotics For Weight Loss

A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study consisted of 64 obese participants aged 18 to 65 who were assigned either to receive daily probiotic supplements containing eight strains of live bacteria (Lactobacillus acidophilus, Lactobacillus casei, Bifidobacterium longum, Bifidobacterium bifidum, Bifidobacterium breve, Inulin, Psyllium Husk Fibre, and Vitamin D3) or a placebo for 12 weeks.
The study found that waist circumference was decreased among those taking the probiotic supplement (- 2 cm), while no changes were observed in hip circumference or serum levels of leptin and ghrelin hormones related to appetite regulation.
Another study conducted in 2017 at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, found that participants who consumed a probiotic supplement containing Lactobacillus acidophilus and Bifidobacterium longum experienced improved energy levels when compared to individuals who took a placebo. Additionally, the study reported that taking this probiotic supplement resulted in greater gastrointestinal comfort during intense physical activities than a placebo.
Yet another study published in 2019 by scientists at Barry University tested the effect of Lactobacillus gasseri on body weight and body fat mass. Over 16 weeks, 84 overweight volunteers were given either a placebo or 1.5 billion colony-forming units of live Lactobacillus gasseri on a daily basis.
Subjects who received the probiotic supplement experienced significant reductions in both body weight and body fat mass when compared to those who received the placebo. The results from this research suggest that regularly taking a probiotic containing Lactobacillus gasseri can potentially lead to a decrease in overall body weight and fat mass over time.
Coming to the bifido strains found in DuoTrim, a study published in the British Journal of Nutrition suggests that the probiotic strain Bifidobacterium animalis subsp. lactis (B. animalis) can help support weight management, reduce body fat and improve energy levels in obese adults.
The participants who were supplemented with probiotics lost more weight than those on placebo and had lower levels of hunger hormones like ghrelin and insulin, suggesting that supplementation can modulate these hormones to reduce appetite and promote weight loss.
