African Mango
African Mango, scientifically known as Irvingia gabonensis, is a fruit native to West Africa. It is commonly referred to as "wild mango" or "bush mango." This tropical fruit is known for its vibrant orange color and unique shape. The African Mango is larger than regular mangoes and has a fibrous flesh, similar to a pumpkin.
A research trial conducted on African Mango demonstrated its potential benefits for blood sugar regulation and energy levels. The results of the study showed that the group taking African Mango extract experienced significant improvements in blood sugar regulation. Their fasting blood sugar levels and insulin resistance were reduced compared to the placebo group.

Gymnema
Gymnema is a woody climbing shrub that is native to the tropical forests of India and Africa. It features long, slender, and oval-shaped leaves arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. The leaves are smooth, glossy, and have a characteristic sweet taste, earning the herb its name "gurmar," or sugar destroyer!
A clinical trial conducted on gymnema's blood sugar-regulating effects involved participants with type 2 diabetes. The results showed a significant decrease in fasting blood sugar levels, postprandial (after meal) blood sugar levels, and HbA1c levels (a measure of long-term blood sugar control) in the participants.
Specifically, fasting blood sugar levels decreased by an average of 24%, postprandial blood sugar levels decreased by 36%, and HbA1c levels decreased by 21%.
Astragalus
Astragalus contains bioactive compounds, such as polysaccharides and saponins, which have been found to exert hypoglycemic effects. These compounds can help enhance insulin sensitivity, promote glucose uptake in cells, and inhibit the breakdown of glycogen, a stored form of glucose in the liver.
In addition to its potential effects on blood glucose levels, astragalus may also aid in weight loss efforts. It contains compounds that have demonstrated anti-obesity properties in animal studies.
While the inclusion of astragalus in Amiclear drops appears promising, it is important to note that further scientific research is needed to fully understand its effects on blood glucose regulation and weight loss.
Coleus
Coelus, also known as Coleus forskohlii, is a powerful herb being used for centuries. This ingredient has been added to Amiclear Drops to provide a comprehensive solution for individuals looking to maintain stable blood sugar levels and achieve healthy weight loss goals.
This herb contains a compound known as forskolin, which has been shown to increase insulin sensitivity. Insulin is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating blood sugar levels. By enhancing insulin sensitivity, Coelus helps the body utilize glucose more effectively, preventing spikes and dips in blood sugar levels.
Furthermore, Coelus has been found to promote weight loss by increasing the activity of an enzyme called adenylate cyclase. This enzyme is involved in the breakdown of stored fat cells, leading to a reduction in body fat.
